Tarpon Tackle

  1. A medium-heavy to extra-heavy rod
  2. Rod length 8 to 12 foot
  3. 5000 to 10000 size spinning reel or casting reel 200-500
  4. Circle hooks 6/0 to 10/0
  5. Lures would be mirrorlure, paddletail, or spook
  6. 50 to 65 pound braided line
  7. 60 to 80 pound flurocarbon leader
  8. Live baits would be shrimp, mullet, or pilchards

Largemouth Tackle

  1. A medium to heavy rod
  2. Rod length 6 to 8 foot
  3. 1000 to 2500 size spinning reel or casting reel 75 to 300
  4. Circle hooks 1/0 to 5/0 or EWG hook 3/0 to 5/0
  5. Lures would be senko, paddletail, or spook
  6. 8 to 65 pound braided line
  7. 6 to 20 pound flurocarbon leader
  8. Live baits would be shad, bluegill, or worms

Red Drum Tackle

  1. A medium to heavy rod
  2. Rod length 6 to 8 foot
  3. 3000 to 8000 size spinning reel or casting reel 200 to 300
  4. Circle hooks 3/0 to 10/0 or EWG hook 3/0 to 8/0
  5. Lures would be mirrorlure, paddletail, or spook
  6. 15 to 65 pound braided line
  7. 10 to 40 pound flurocarbon leader
  8. Live baits would be pinfish, shrimp, or mullet
